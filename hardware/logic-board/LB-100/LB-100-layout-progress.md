# LB-100 Controlled Layout Progress

Status: `EVT-LAYOUT-AUTHORIZED` — RF keepout and reference-plane intent exist; routing remains open

## Completed Milestone

- Generated the controlled four-layer `kicad/LB-100.kicad_pcb` from the
  reviewed 104-component schematic and project-local footprints.
- Applied the 100 mm x 70 mm outline, four local M2.5 holes and four shared
  PB/LB stack holes around the centered back-side JPB1 connector.
- Placed MCU, rail conversion, JFB1, service interfaces, microSD, BLE, IMU,
  lux sensor, ten-channel control/IMON support and the independent
  `FOG_A_SW_IN`/`FOG_B_SW_IN` protection chains in the reviewed functional
  zones.
- Added accessible top-side E73 recovery pads for module `SWDIO`, `SWDCLK`,
  reset, switched 3.3 V target reference and GND; all five are routed and
  excluded from the assembly BOM.
- Moved U7 to the `+Y` board edge with its ceramic antenna facing outwards and
  added one exact rule area spanning `F.Cu`, `In1.Cu`, `In2.Cu`, and `B.Cu`.
  The boundary is documented in `LB-100-e73-antenna-keepout.md`; tracks, vias,
  pads and copper pours are forbidden in it.
- Added one GND-zone intent on each copper layer. A KiCad 10.0.4 refill audit
  confirms that the zones respect the E73 rule area and reduce the physical
  connectivity backlog without introducing shorts or clearance violations.
- Added deterministic generation, preliminary USB rules and CI validation.
- Completed a controlled four-layer signal-routing iteration. The route data is stored in
  `kicad/LB-100-routing.csv` and regenerated by CI.
- KiCad reports no copper shorts, track crossings, clearance violations, edge
  violations, courtyard overlaps or mounting-hole conflicts in the routed
  milestone.
- Schematic parity is exact apart from H1-H8, which are board-only mechanical
  holes. The current post-refill open-connectivity count is tracked directly by
  `tools/validate_lb100_layout.py`; those items remain the explicit manual
  fan-out and critical-interface routing backlog.
